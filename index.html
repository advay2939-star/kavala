Dispense

STEP 02

Massage or Swish

Massage gently along the gumline, or swish a small amount as part of your oral-care ritual.

STEP 03

Spit & Continue

Spit out. Do not swallow. Continue brushing, flossing, and visiting your dentist as advised.
